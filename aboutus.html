About Us

Preferred Dentist is centered around helping you achieve the best dental care possible. We focus on the importance of oral health going hand in hand with your overall fitness plan. Our motto is helping you make the best body you can from teeth to toes and we mean it. We have partnered with other fitness and health conscious organizations to offer you tips on healthy eating, exercise and positive progression towards your fitness goals. We are all about the battle against germs and bacteria.

We offer a sonic toothbrush with a UV sanitizer for the brush heads at a reasonable cost via Amazon. We have researched and partnered with ZoonoUSA to provide products that fight bacteria via a 24 hour hand sanitizer. Taking it a step further we invested in products that break down the bacteria of body odor, acne and foot fungus to help you maintain your own personal cleanliness. We put all this into a travel kit with a mini sonic toothbrush so you have what you need if you are on the go. Lastly, we have begun locating dentists who can help you with regular dental check ups and care as needed.

Our dentists become Preferred Dentists based on specific guidelines revolving around good patient care and quality. We are focused on you, the individual and helping you reach your health and fitness goals.
